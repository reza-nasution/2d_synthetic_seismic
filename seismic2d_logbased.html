AI profiles follow the digitized DeGray East GR log character (Slatt & Stone, 2010) scaled laterally by zone geometry: AI(x,d) = AI_shale + f(x) × (AI_log(d) − AI_shale), where f(x) is the lateral sand fraction derived from the selected geometry.
